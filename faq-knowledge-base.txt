Campus Services FAQ Knowledge Base
Document Name: Uniserve FAQ v1.0

# 1. Laundry Services
Q: How does the laundry service work?
A: You can schedule a pickup through the Uniserve app. We come to your dorm, pick up your clothes, wash, dry, fold them, and return them within 24 hours. [1]

Q: How much does laundry cost?
A: Our standard wash & fold service is $1.50 per pound. We also offer a monthly subscription for $45/month which covers up to 40 pounds of laundry. [1]

Q: What if I have delicate items?
A: Please place delicate items in a separate bag inside your main laundry bag and leave a note in the app. These items will be air-dried. [1]

# 2. Grooming Services
Q: Where are the grooming services located?
A: We partner with several local barbers and salons right off-campus. You can book an appointment directly through the Uniserve app and show your student ID for a 15% discount. [2]

Q: Can someone come to my dorm for a haircut?
A: Yes! We have a select group of mobile barbers who can come to your dorm common area. You can filter by "Mobile Barber" in the booking section. [2]

# 3. Tech Support
Q: My laptop won't turn on. Can you help?
A: Yes. Our student-led IT support team can run diagnostics on your laptop. Submit a ticket in the app, and someone will meet you at the campus library tech desk. [3]

Q: Do you repair phone screens?
A: Yes, we provide screen repair services for most major smartphone brands. Screen repairs typically take 2-4 hours and start at $75 depending on the model. [3]

# 4. Food Delivery
Q: What restaurants do you deliver from?
A: We deliver from all dining halls on campus, as well as our 15 partnered restaurants on Main Street. [4]

Q: How late do you deliver food?
A: Our student dashers deliver until 2:00 AM on weekdays, and 3:00 AM on weekends to fuel your late-night study sessions. [4]
